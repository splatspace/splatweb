Welcome to SplatSpace

Since 2010, SplatSpace has been a multidisciplinary workshop and makerspace in downtown Durham, NC. We are a non-profit, member driven, member funded organization.

We provide our members with the following:

  • Wood Shop
  • Laser Cutters
  • Electrionics Stations
  • Large Format Printer
  • Crafting Station
  • Metal Shop
  • 3D Printers
  • Vinyl Cutter
  • Power Tools
  • Project/Material Storage

Our community meets

SplatSpace members hold regular meetings on the first Tuesday of each month, which are open to visitors. We encourage anyone interested in visiting or becoming a member to join us at a meeting, or request a tour by contacting us at info@splatspace.org.
